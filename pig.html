PIG DICE

Instructions for the game

    Each turn a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "hold": 1)If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player,s turn. 2)If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their turn total & the player's turn continues. 3)If a player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to tjheir score, & it becomes the next player's turn. The first player to score 100 or more points wins.
